My Te Whare Tapa Whā

Wall Activies
Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing) Exercising 6x per week. 3x gym, 3x running.
Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing) I live away from family. I will schedule time to call my parents and siblings. In addition to having meals with my chosen family in Wellington.
Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing) Balance study with musical outlets. Breaks and going easy on myself.
Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing) Meditation before study. My spirituality is also tied together with acknowledging my musical outlets and creativity.
Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment) Weekly bush walks and swims (if it's warm enough)
