My Reflections

What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the Human Skills learning?

I’m anxious about finding answers to problems as soon as possible. And if it doesn’t happen, I start to feel like I’m not smart enough.

What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

I feel like these roles particularly apply to your view of yourself. It’s about discovering the best way that you learn, and creating a healthy environment for your learning. Self-awareness to know your limits and habits. Empathy to yourself and others while struggling with tasks. And creating positive values to reflect you as a person, and to create a healthy working environment for yourself or your team.

What has surprised you the most about the Human Skills learning?

The fixed & growth mindset/Neuroplasticity learnings. I thought I knew what category I fell into but the internal findings were surprising to me. I realized that I had been in a fixed mindset for particular things regarding this course.

What were the most challenging aspects of the Human Skills learning?

Honestly the most challenging this was trying to put aside quality time to engage with this material. I’ve had a lot of anxiety about falling behind in this course so at the beginning I didn’t spend probably enough time on this part of the course, and instead focused on the tech. But around sprint 3 I realized that it’s actually so crucial to learning, and particularly in this course.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on Human Skills learning in a web development Bootcamp course?

Because there is an unbelievable amount of learning taking place in a short period of time. If the mind isn’t in a functional state and unable to take in a process new information, it would be significantly more challenging than normal.

Does the time you spent studying Human Skills learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practice programming instead? Justify your answer.

Initially I wanted to spend more time on the tech, but now I prioritize the Human Skills. It’s an extremely valuable resource to have.
