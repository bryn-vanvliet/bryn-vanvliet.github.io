Bootcamp Learning Plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

My long term career goal is to obtain a remote software development job ideally in the arts field. More specifically, I would like to work for a software company that provides programs to musicians like myself. For example; Sibelius, Serrato, Rekordbox etc. I feel that my experience as a musician would be helpful in that context. I would also like to build apps as a potential passive income stream.

What are my biggest strengths and limitations going to be at Bootcamp?

Problem solving and rememebering syntax

What will be my biggest non-techincal challenge at bootcamp?

Remaining motivated through challening times. Keeping up with the coursework.

What non-technical skills - human skills - would I like to see developed in myself while at Dev Academy?

I'd like to improve my self-awareness. Specifically in terms of managing feelings of overwhelm. Currently, I don't realise i'm experiencing great overwhelm until it's too late.

What are my expectations from the Bootcamp team?

To be patient and helpful. I'd like the team to help me to find my own answers through guidance if I am stuck. That way I can navigate my learning better.

My expectations of myself and my commitment

  • I will manage myself by developing and adhering to my personal study routine. Currently that involves a mix of set starting times for the day, exercise and mental health breaks.
  • When experiencing overwhelm, i will take my foot of the gas temporarily, step back and perhaps do an activity that takes my attention and energy off the task. I will also reach out to my facilitator and cohort.
  • When needing help, i will first use the resources supplied by Dev Academy, information on the internet and chat gbt. And also my facilitator and cohort. I fortunately have friends who have done the course recently too, so can also reach out to them for some more help.
  • From the facilitators i expect timely responses and quality advice to resolve problems efficiently. I also hope that the facilitators will help me to resolve problems by guiding me to figure out the answers myself.
  • With navigating other commitments around this course, i’m still active as a musicians, so scheduling practice time in is super helpful to keep my software developer brain fresh. I also have exercise times blocked out each day. I will aim to keep weekends free, unless I need to catch up.
